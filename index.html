About


LETHALBOTS

Get ready to witness the ultimate clash of robotics as SLTC Research University hosts the highly anticipated Lethal Bots 2024 competition. This event promises to be an exhilarating showcase of engineering prowess, innovation, and strategic thinking. Join us in the arena where the future of robotic comes alive. Let's embark on this thrilling journey together!

Rules and Regulations

Eligibility: University Students Only ▼
Participants must be university or higher education students, ensuring an educational competitive environment.
Bot Mobility: Flexible Movement Options ▼
Bots can use wheels, tracks, or flight, encouraging innovative designs within arena safety.
Weapons: Allowed and Prohibited Types ▼
Multiple weapons allowed, but dangerous weapons like fire and explosives are banned to ensure safety.
Do the participants need to attend physically? ▼
No, the event can be attended online.
Size/Weight: 40x40x60cm, 13kg Limit ▼
Bots must fit within the specified dimensions and weight for competition fairness and safety.
Activation: 30-Second Start/Stop ▼
Bots should activate or deactivate within 30 seconds, ensuring controlled operation and safety.
Safety: Protective Covers Required ▼
Sharp edges need covers; bots stay in designated areas to avoid injuries.
Control System: RC/Bluetooth, Fail-Safe ▼
Use RC or Bluetooth; bot and weapons stop if the transmitter fails for safety.
